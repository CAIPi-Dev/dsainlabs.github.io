DSAIN Reliability Lens

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

DSAIN Research Labs ("we", "us", "our") develops the DSAIN Reliability Lens browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our extension handles — or, more accurately, does not handle — your data.

DSAIN Reliability Lens collects zero user data. Nothing is transmitted, stored, tracked, or shared. Ever.

What the Extension Does

The extension analyzes AI chatbot responses displayed on supported websites (such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) and displays a reliability grade based on linguistic pattern analysis. This analysis determines how much the output's expressed confidence is supported by evidence, source citations, and appropriate caveats.

How It Works

All processing occurs entirely within your browser using JavaScript bundled in the extension package. When you visit a supported AI chatbot website, the extension reads the text content of AI-generated responses from the page, runs a local analysis algorithm, and injects a small reliability indicator into the page. The analyzed text is processed in memory and immediately discarded. It is never stored, cached, logged, or transmitted.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit. This extension does not collect, access, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • Your conversations or prompts sent to AI chatbots
  • The AI chatbot responses analyzed by the extension
  • Your browsing history, bookmarks, or tabs
  • Cookies, local storage, or session data
  • Passwords, credentials, or authentication tokens
  • Location, IP address, or device information
  • Clicks, keystrokes, mouse movements, or scroll behavior
  • Health, financial, or any other personal information
  • Usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports

Network Requests

The extension makes zero network requests. No data is sent to DSAIN Research Labs servers, third-party services, analytics platforms, or any external destination. The extension operates entirely offline once installed.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to read AI chatbot response text on the currently active tab and inject the reliability grade badge. This permission only activates on supported AI chatbot websites.
  • Host permissions (content_scripts): The extension runs only on specific AI chatbot domains (chatgpt.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, copilot.microsoft.com, perplexity.ai, poe.com). It does not run on any other websites.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services. There are no embedded trackers, advertising networks, analytics tools, or external scripts of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The extension does not collect data from any user, including children. Since no data is collected, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how this extension handles data — for example, by introducing optional features that require network access — we will update this privacy policy before those changes take effect. Any version of the extension that collects data will require explicit user consent before any data processing occurs.
