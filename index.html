Evidence Grounding Layer

Every factual claim tagged with source provenance. Tiered verification: Compustat via Python → SEC 10-K risk factors via Neo4j → Tavily web research → training knowledge. EGR computed automatically.

Tier 1: Compustat (Python FinancialModel)
Tier 2: SEC risk factors (Neo4j)
Metric: Evidence Grounding Ratio (EGR)

Dual Confidence Decomposition

Every output assessed on two dimensions: expressed confidence (how certain it sounds) vs. warranted confidence (how much evidence supports it). The gap is the ARP manifesting.

Warranted: Epistemic "weight"

Research Foundation

This isn't a weekend project. It's a multi-year research program.

DSAIN is built on doctoral research into the governance of AI-augmented strategic decision-making. The theoretical framework, the experimental design, and the working system are inseparable — the artifact embodies the theory.

The Augmentation-Reliability Paradox (ARP) posits that AI systems that most effectively relax bounded rationality simultaneously create conditions where bounded reliability failures are most consequential. The same features that make AI articulate and capable make it prone to fabrication and sycophancy.

Target: Academy of Management Journal / Organization Science
Design & Build Empirical contribution. Design-science instantiation of the ARP framework as a fully operational six-agent strategic decision engine. Within-subject ablation across 12–15 S&P 500 firms using Compustat-grounded financial data.
Paper 3 — Governance Architecture Ablation: How Microfoundational Controls Shape AI Decision Quality
Target: Strategic Management Journal / MIS Quarterly
Planned Methodological contribution. Systematic ablation of seven governance microfoundations (GM1–GM7) to isolate causal effects on decision quality, evidence grounding, and confidence calibration. Includes dual confidence metric grounded in Griffin & Tversky (1992).
